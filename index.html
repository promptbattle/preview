Closed Preview Rules Summary

We look for unique creative styles in NightCafe artists. Structure your parameters based on our mechanical guidelines:

Factions Design alignments (like CLIP Resistance or Newbies), reflecting community identity.
Passives Traits triggering automatically (such as copying tags or taking opponent visibility).
Signature (Ults) High-impact active mechanics costing both Creator Loyalty and Reputation.
Favourite Prompt A unique prompt phrase that awards bonus stats (e.g. +1 Quality or Visibility).
